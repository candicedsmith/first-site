Lost in Translation

My love of translated works of fiction

I have a love of works of translated foreign fiction, and reading stories from another culture's viewpoint. Currently I am reading a series called On the Calculation of Volume by Danish author Solvej Balle. The story centres around a woman who is stuck in time and keeps waking up on the 18th of November. There are four books published out of a series of seven (of which I have three), with the other three still being written.

Her first book was shortlisted for the Booker Prize.


Other works of translated fiction I have enjoyed reading

  • We by Yevgeny Zamyatin (Russia)
  • Drive Your Plough Over the Bones of the Dead by Olga Tokarczuk (Poland)
  • The Forbidden Notebook by Alba de Céspedes (Italy)
  • Before the Rocket by Loranne Vella (Malta)
